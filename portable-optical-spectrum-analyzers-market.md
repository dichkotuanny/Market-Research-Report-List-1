<p><p>The Portable Optical Spectrum Analyzers market is divided into high frequency and low frequency measurement types. High frequency measurement focuses on analyzing optical signals with rapid modulation rates, often used in advanced telecommunications and data transmission applications. These analyzers provide precise readings of wavelength and power for signals in the gigahertz range. Conversely, low frequency measurement pertains to signals with slower modulation, typically utilized in industrial applications and research settings, offering insights into broader wavelength bands and signal stability.</p></p>
