<p><p>The MBR membrane market is categorized into three primary types: tubular membranes, hollow fiber membranes, and flat membranes. Tubular membranes consist of cylindrical structures that offer high filtration efficiency and easy cleaning, making them suitable for industrial applications. Hollow fiber membranes, with their high surface area and compact design, are ideal for residential and small-scale uses. Flat membranes, characterized by their large surface area and modular design, are commonly used in various wastewater treatment processes, providing flexibility and efficiency.</p></p>
