<p><p>The gonorrhea testing market includes several methods: </p><p>1. **Nucleic Acid Amplification (NAA)** detects bacterial DNA, offering high sensitivity and specificity. </p><p>2. **Gram Stain** identifies the bacteria through microscopic examination, primarily used in males. </p><p>3. **Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA)** measures antibodies, providing indirect evidence of infection. </p><p>4. **Gonorrhea Culture** allows bacteria growth for detection and antibiotic sensitivity testing. </p><p>5. **Rapid tests** deliver quick results, often using immunological methods for immediate diagnosis, benefiting early treatment and control.</p></p>
