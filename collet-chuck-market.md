<p><p>A collet chuck is a precision tool holding device widely used in machining operations to securely grip cylindrical workpieces. It operates by using a specially designed collet that expands or contracts when tightened or loosened, providing strong clamping forces while ensuring high accuracy and minimal runout. These chucks are favored in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing due to their efficiency and ability to handle high-speed operations.</p><p>The Collet Chuck Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.50% during the forecast period.
