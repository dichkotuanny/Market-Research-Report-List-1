<p><p>The freeze drying technology market comprises various types, including Tray-Style, Manifold, and Rotary Freeze Drying. Tray-Style freeze dryers utilize flat trays for uniform product placement and efficient moisture removal, ideal for bulk processing. Manifold freeze drying employs glass vessels that allow for simultaneous drying of multiple samples, enhancing versatility. Rotary freeze drying, or shell freeze drying, features rotating drums to maximize surface area exposure, improving heat and mass transfer. Together, these technologies cater to diverse applications in pharmaceuticals, food, and biotechnology.</p></p>
