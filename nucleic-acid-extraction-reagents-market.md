<p><p>The nucleic acid extraction reagents market includes various chemicals critical for isolating DNA and RNA. Proteinase K is an enzyme that digests proteins to release nucleic acids. NaCl is used to stabilize nucleic acid interactions, while CTAB/NaCl solution facilitates the extraction of polysaccharides along with nucleic acids. Chloroform/Isoamyl Alcohol and Phenol/Chloroform/Isoamyl Alcohol mixtures are organic solvents that separate nucleic acids from proteins and debris. Isopropanol and Ethanol precipitate nucleic acids for purification, enhancing yield and quality for downstream applications.</p></p>
