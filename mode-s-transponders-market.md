<p><strong>The Mode S Transponders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>1030 MHz</li><li>1090 MHz</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mode S transponders operate in two primary frequency bands: 1030 MHz and 1090 MHz. The 1030 MHz frequency is used for interrogating aircraft, allowing ground systems to request identification and additional data. Conversely, 1090 MHz is utilized for transponder replies, where aircraft send back information, such as altitude and identification codes. The 1090 MHz market is more widespread globally, often found in commercial aviation, while 1030 MHz is crucial for military operations and some specialized systems.</p></p>
