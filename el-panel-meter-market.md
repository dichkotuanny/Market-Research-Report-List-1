<p><p>The EL panel meter market is divided into two primary types: analogue and digital EL panel meters. Analogue EL panel meters display measurements through a needle on a dial, providing a visual representation of readings, often favored for their simplicity and real-time response. Digital EL panel meters, on the other hand, showcase readings on a digital display, offering higher accuracy, data tracking, and ease of interpretation. Both types serve essential roles in monitoring electrical parameters across various industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
