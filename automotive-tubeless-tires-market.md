<p><p>The automotive tubeless tire market is primarily divided into two types: radial tubeless tires and bias tubeless tires. Radial tubeless tires feature layers of fabric that run radially from the center, enhancing flexibility and performance while providing better fuel efficiency and handling. In contrast, bias tubeless tires have layers arranged in a crisscross pattern, offering a sturdier build suitable for heavy loads but generally less efficient in terms of fuel economy and handling. Each type serves distinct driving needs and vehicle applications.</p></p>
