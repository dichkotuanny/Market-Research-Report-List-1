<p><p>The EVA wax market can be segmented into two primary types: non-oxidized and oxidized EVA waxes. Non-oxidized EVA waxes are characterized by their high viscosity and thermal stability, making them suitable for applications in coatings, adhesives, and plastics. In contrast, oxidized EVA waxes, produced through controlled oxidation processes, exhibit improved compatibility with polar resins and serve as ideal additives in inks and surface coatings. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing performance and properties in various industries.</p></p>
