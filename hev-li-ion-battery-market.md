<p><p>The HEV Li-ion battery market comprises several types, each featuring unique chemistry and performance attributes. Lithium Manganese Oxide (LMO) offers thermal stability and high power output, while Lithium Iron Phosphate (LiFePO4) is known for safety and longevity. Lithium Nickel Manganese Cobalt Oxide (NMC) balances energy density and cost, making it popular in electric vehicles. Lithium Nickel Cobalt Aluminum Oxide (NCA) excels in energy density but at a higher cost. Lastly, Lithium Titanate Oxide (LTO) provides rapid charging capabilities and long life cycles, although with lower energy density.</p></p>
