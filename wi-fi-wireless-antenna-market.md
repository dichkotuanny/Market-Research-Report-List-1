<p><ul><li>Omni directional antennas</li><li>Directional Antennas</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wi-Fi wireless antenna market comprises two primary types: omnidirectional and directional antennas. Omnidirectional antennas radiate signals uniformly in all directions, making them ideal for broad coverage in environments like homes or offices. In contrast, directional antennas focus signals in a specific direction, providing enhanced range and strength, suitable for point-to-point connections or areas with obstacles. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to varying needs for coverage, range, and signal strength within wireless communication systems.</p></p>
