<p><p>Chemiluminescence Detectors (CLD) are analytical instruments used to detect trace amounts of gases by measuring the light emitted during chemical reactions, typically involving nitric oxide. The market can be segmented into two main types: Mono CLD and Dual CLD. Mono CLD offers a single measurement channel for detecting specific compounds, while Dual CLD features two channels, enabling simultaneous detection of multiple compounds, improving sensitivity and accuracy. Both types are vital in environmental monitoring and industrial applications for pollutant analysis.</p></p>
