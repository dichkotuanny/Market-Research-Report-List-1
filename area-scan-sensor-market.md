<p><p>Area scan sensors are essential in various imaging applications, categorized by their spectral sensitivity. Mono (monochrome) sensors capture images in grayscale, ideal for high-resolution and detailed analysis without color distractions. GBR (green, blue, red) sensors utilize a color filter array to produce full-color images, offering accurate color reproduction suitable for tasks requiring detailed color analysis. NIR (near-infrared) sensors can detect infrared light, making them valuable in agricultural, industrial, and security applications, where visibility beyond the human eye is crucial.</p></p>
