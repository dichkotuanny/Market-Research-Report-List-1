Graphite plates are known for their excellent electrical conductivity and corrosion resistance but have limitations in mechanical strength. Metal plates offer higher durability and lighter weight but may be prone to corrosion. Composite plates combine the advantages of both, providing a balance of strength, conductivity, and weight, catering to diverse fuel cell applications.</p></p>
