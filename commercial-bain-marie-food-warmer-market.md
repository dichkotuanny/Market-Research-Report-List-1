<p><p>The commercial Bain Marie food warmer market is categorized into two main types: wet heat and dry heat Bain Marie food warmers. Wet heat Bain Maries use a water bath to maintain food at a stable temperature, ideal for sauces and soup, preventing them from drying out. In contrast, dry heat Bain Maries utilize heated air, suitable for maintaining the temperature of solid foods like casseroles and meats.
