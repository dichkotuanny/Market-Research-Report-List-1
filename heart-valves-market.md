<p><p>The heart valves market encompasses three main types: Transcatheter Heart Valves, Tissue Heart Valves, and Mechanical Heart Valves. Transcatheter Heart Valves are minimally invasive devices used for procedures such as TAVR, offering quicker recovery. Tissue Heart Valves, made from biological materials, are favored for their natural function and lower thrombosis risk, but require replacement after a decade. Mechanical Heart Valves, crafted from durable materials, provide longevity, though they necessitate lifelong anticoagulation therapy to prevent blood clots.</p></p>
