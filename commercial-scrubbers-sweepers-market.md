<p><p>The commercial scrubbers and sweepers market includes three primary types: riding type, walking type, and self-operated models. Self-operated scrubbers and sweepers, often equipped with automation features, enhance productivity by reducing operator involvement, making them suitable for various commercial cleaning needs.</p></p>
