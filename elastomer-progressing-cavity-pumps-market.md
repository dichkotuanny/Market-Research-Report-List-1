<p><p>Elastomer Progressing Cavity Pumps are specialized mechanical devices designed to transport fluids with varying viscosities, including highly viscous and abrasive materials. They operate using a helical rotor paired with a stator, creating defined cavities that move the fluid through the pump as the rotor turns.
