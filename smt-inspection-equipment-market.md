<p><strong>The SMT Inspection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AOI</li><li>SPI</li><li>AXI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SMT inspection equipment market comprises three main types: Automated Optical Inspection (AOI), Solder Paste Inspection (SPI), and Automated X-ray Inspection (AXI). AOI utilizes high-resolution cameras to detect surface defects on printed circuit boards during assembly. SPI focuses on verifying solder paste application before component placement, ensuring optimal solder quantity and placement accuracy. AXI employs X-ray technology to inspect hidden solder joints and complex assemblies, providing insight into the internal structure of the PCB for quality assurance.</p></p>
