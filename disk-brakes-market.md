<p><strong>The Disk Brakes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Opposed Piston Type</li><li>Floating Caliper Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disk brakes market primarily includes two types: opposed piston type and floating caliper type. Opposed piston brakes feature two pistons acting on either side of the brake pad for uniform pressure, offering enhanced stopping power and heat dissipation, ideal for high-performance vehicles. In contrast, floating caliper brakes utilize a single piston and a caliper that moves with the brake pad, providing a lighter design and ease of installation, commonly used in mass-market vehicles for cost-efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
