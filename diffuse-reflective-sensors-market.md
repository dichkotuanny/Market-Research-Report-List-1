<p><strong>The Diffuse Reflective Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal Diffuse Sensors</li><li>Diffuse Sensors with Background Suppression</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diffuse reflective sensors detect objects by emitting light and measuring the reflection from surfaces. Normal diffuse sensors respond to any reflected light, making them suitable for various applications where precise distance measurement isn't critical. In contrast, diffuse sensors with background suppression are designed to differentiate between the target object and background, allowing for accurate detection even in cluttered environments. This enhances their functionality in applications requiring high precision, such as industrial automation and robotics. Both types cater to different operational needs.</p></p>
