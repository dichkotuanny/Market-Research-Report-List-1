<p><p>The Electret Condenser Microphone (ECM) market is divided into two primary types: analog and digital. Analog ECMs convert sound waves into electrical signals directly, providing simplicity and compatibility with various audio equipment. They are typically used in applications like speech recording and broadcasting. Digital ECMs, on the other hand, convert audio signals into digital format, offering enhanced sound quality and features such as noise reduction and signal processing. This makes them suitable for advanced applications like smartphones and professional audio equipment.</p></p>
