<p><p>The electrochemical instrumentation market encompasses a range of devices used for measuring and analyzing electrochemical properties. Potentiostats and galvanostats control and measure voltage and current in electrochemical cells. Ion chromatographs separate ions for analysis, while conductivity meters gauge solution conductivity. Salinity meters specifically measure salt concentration, whereas titrators determine chemical concentrations through controlled addition of reactants. pH meters assess acidity or alkalinity. Other instruments in this market include voltammeters and electrochemical sensors, catering to diverse analytical needs.</p></p>
