<p><p>The air conditioning equipment market comprises various types, each serving specific needs. Package air conditioners are self-contained units ideal for larger spaces, combining all components in a single unit. Split air conditioners feature indoor and outdoor units, providing efficient cooling with quieter operation. Chiller air conditioners are used in commercial settings, circulating chilled water for large-scale cooling. Window air conditioners are compact, window-mounted units suitable for single rooms, offering a cost-effective solution for localized cooling requirements.</p></p>
