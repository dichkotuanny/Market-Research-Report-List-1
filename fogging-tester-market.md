<p><strong>The Fogging Tester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gloss Method</li><li>Gravimetric Method</li><li>Haze Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fogging Tester market comprises three main types: Gloss Method, Gravimetric Method, and Haze Method. The Gloss Method assesses the reflective properties of surfaces, providing insights into quality by measuring sheen. The Gravimetric Method determines the mass of condensed moisture formed on the surface, indicating fogging resistance. The Haze Method evaluates light transmission distortion, quantifying how much fog affects clarity. Together, these methods are vital for industries prioritizing optical clarity and material performance in various applications.</p></p>
