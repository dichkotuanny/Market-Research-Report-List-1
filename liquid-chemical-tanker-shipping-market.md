<p><p>The liquid chemical tanker shipping market encompasses three main types: Inland, Coastal, and Deep-Sea chemical tankers. Inland chemical tankers operate on rivers and inland waterways, facilitating regional transport of chemicals. Coastal chemical tankers serve shorter maritime routes along coastlines, connecting ports and facilitating regional trade. Deep-Sea chemical tankers are designed for international shipping, covering long distances to transport bulk chemicals across oceans. Each type caters to specific logistical needs based on distance, volume, and regulatory considerations in transporting hazardous materials.</p></p>
