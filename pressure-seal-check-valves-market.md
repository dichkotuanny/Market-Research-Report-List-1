<p><p>The Pressure Seal Check Valves market primarily features two types of connection methods: socket weld connections and butt weld connections. Socket weld connections involve a pipe being inserted into a valve's socket for a secure fit, promoting ease of installation and maintenance. In contrast, butt weld connections require the ends of pipes to be joined directly to the valve, ensuring enhanced strength and reduced leakage. Both methods cater to different application needs, offering reliability and performance in high-pressure systems.</p></p>
